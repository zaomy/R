Dataset:  nat_fib_comp.dat

Source: A. Athijayamani, M. Thiruchitrambalam, V. Manikandan, B. Pazhanivel
(2010). "Mechanical Properties of Natural Fibers Reinforced Polyester Hybrid
Composite," International Journal of Plastics Technology," Vol. 14, pp104-116.

Description: Results of experiment relating tensile strength and flexural 
strength of composite to fiber length and fiber content. Models fit:

TS = A0*(FL^A1)*(FC^A2)
FS = B0*(FL^B1)*(FC^B2)

Variables/Columns
Fiber Length (cm)  7-8
Fiber Content  (wt%)  15-16
Tensile Strength  (MPa)  20-24
Flexural Strength (MPa)  28-32
