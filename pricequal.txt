Dataset: pricequal.dat
Source: McConnell (1968). An Experimental Examination of the Price-Quality
Relationship. The Journal of Business. Vol.41 #4, pp 439-444

Description: Ordinal Ratings of Three Beers of Different Prices (Actually
the same brand). 60 subjects each tasted each brand, spread over time
in different orders.

Variables/Columns
Price   1  (3=High,2=Medium,1=Low)
Quality Rating   3  (0=undrinkable,1=Poor,2=Fair,3=Good,4=Very Pleasant)
Count   5-6  (number of Respondents)
